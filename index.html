  1. A Setswana word originating from the word tlhôlêga, tlhôlêga meaning to create. Tlhôlêgô alludes to that which has been created, or come into existance.

plants and other useful things

Our succulents are home grown atop the highest hill in Kensington, Johannesburg where the air is clean and the sun is plenty. No artificial fetilisers or pesticides are used, we value bees and other thriving things. We bring new life to pots that we source from various locations where they are no longer valued.

Our useful things made of shweshwe are considered deeply and are upcycled cotton fabric scraps sourced locally. Our useful things are hand crafted with lots of lovely thoughts and whispers of warm well wishes.

About

We are a father daughter team who have transformed our initmate conversations about life and its meaning over many years, into tangible things we can share with anyone who feels like being shared with.

Tlholego tsa rona. Nature is ours. Not because we made it. It is not concerned with us. It is ours because we are a part of it. And part of it determines what happens to everything we care about. So it, by nature is one of those thing that we care about.

Imphepho has been chosen as the symbol of tlholego. Imphepho is the haze that has guided traditional medicinal practices in South Africa for centuaries. As a herb of healing it inspires us to recover our relationship with creation. Through conscious consumption and giving back where we can.
